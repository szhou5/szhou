Price Chart

Price + SMA

A Simple Moving Average takes the average closing price over a fixed number of periods, then rolls forward one data point at a time. That smoothing helps show the underlying trend, but it also means the line lags price because it is built from past data. In this chart, the 20-day SMA reacts faster while the 50-day SMA shows the broader trend.

RSI

14-day

The Relative Strength Index is a momentum oscillator that measures the speed and change of price moves on a scale from 0 to 100. Traders often watch 70 as an overbought zone and 30 as an oversold zone, while also using RSI to judge whether momentum is strengthening, weakening, or diverging from price.
